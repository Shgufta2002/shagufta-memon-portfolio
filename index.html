ConnectAble: Inclusive Social & Dating Platform

Product Strategy & UX Lead

Executive Summary

ConnectAble is a pioneering social and dating platform designed specifically for individuals with disabilities, creating meaningful connections through innovative accessibility features, intelligent matching algorithms, and comprehensive safety protocols. By addressing the significant gap in inclusive dating technology, ConnectAble empowers users to build authentic relationships while prioritizing dignity, autonomy, and genuine connection.

61M+ US Adults with Disabilities
$13T Global Spending Power
70% Feel Excluded from Dating Apps

Problem Statement & Market Opportunity

Accessibility Barriers

Mainstream platforms lack screen reader compatibility, visual contrast options, and motor accessibility features

Matching Inadequacy

Generic algorithms fail to account for disability-specific compatibility factors and communication preferences

Safety Concerns

Lack of disability-aware safety features and community moderation systems

Social Stigma

Limited spaces for authentic self-expression without fear of discrimination

Key Product Features & Innovation

Accessibility-First Design
  • Multi-modal navigation (voice, gesture, switch control)
  • Customizable visual settings and audio descriptions
  • Screen reader optimization with ARIA labels
  • Haptic feedback for tactile communication
Intelligent Matching Algorithm
  • Disability-informed compatibility scoring
  • Communication preference matching
  • Adaptive learning from user feedback
  • Activity and mobility alignment
Safety & Privacy Features
  • Multi-tier verification with disability accommodation
  • Granular disclosure settings
  • Emergency contact integration
  • Community vouching system
Communication Enhancement
  • Text-to-speech and speech-to-text integration
  • Symbol and emoji communication boards
  • Real-time sign language translation
  • Simplified language options
